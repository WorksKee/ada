What is ICPM?

ICPM is a platform where it covers local and international market, you will have the exclusive chance to meet one on one with the key authorities and decision makers from all GCCand MENAcountries and overseas in healthcare sector.

ICPM is covering Medical, Medical Tourism, Insurance, pharmacy education, Pharma industry , regulatory affairs, Bioequivalence studies, Pharmacovigilance, medicine pricing, dental laboratories, dental, diagnostic labs, and GMP department, genetics, Veterinary, Aesthetics, derma, beauty and cosmetics, accessibilities and rehabilitation.

Moreover you will encounter leading industry experts, consultant , healthcare provider and CEO’s in the healthcare field in private sessions and B2B.

Why the UAE — and Why Now?

Every year ICPM ties up with different success story, and after the success in last 13 events in UAE, this year ICPM is collaborating with government authority under one platform.

Direction and Vision of UAE is to improve and provide the world class healthcare industry, The healthcare scope and gain the trust of healthcare authorities and healthcare providers.

UAE’s economy has seen an unprecedented growth over the past few years which are the result of policies that investor friendly like cuttingred tape and expanding of economic and commerce in UAE considering its importance and the support extended from UAE Rulers, ICPM decided to take the opportunity and organize 14ᵗʰ edition of ICPM at UAE.

Purpose & Objectives of ICPM

  • promote a healthier lifestyle as well as to prevent lifestyle related diseases.
  • Connect the multinational pharmaceutical & medical companies, distributors and retailers with UAE & GCC Regulatory Authorities
  • To highlight the awareness about mass gathering medicine
  • Make a strong connection between the universities, manufacturers, distributors and retailers for the benefit of healthcare
  • Promote, support and Facilitate the Medical tourism in UAE
  • Provide world class treatment to patient with low cost
  • Consolidate efforts between scientific organizations and Healthcare Companies
  • Share the updated information on the latest technology
  • Promote new business and technology ideas in the healthcare sector
  • Make a strong connection between the Private and Government hospitals
  • To increase access to the decentralizing public health system by establishing new infrastructure in existing institutions
  • Developing the domestic pharmaceutical industries and hospitals

Future Plan
